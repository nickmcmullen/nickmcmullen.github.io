About

 

Work

I am currently the Manager, Total Rewards at Tanium, the global leader in converged endpoint management (XEM).

I ensure Tanium’s day-to-day compensation operations, cyclical programs, and recognition initiatives run smoothly and support the organization’s global rewards philosophy. Responsibilities include evaluating and benchmarking jobs, developing career frameworks, recommending packages for internal transfers and promotions, compensation consultation and education, creating and managing compensation project plans, writing and proposing budgets for merit and equity review cycles, configuring HR technology solutions for effective performance during compensation cycles, consulting with workforce planning/people analytics on workforce strategy, and compensation data management including pay grade assignments and survey participation.
